🧠 Course Format

EDUS 268 is a hybrid-synchronous course designed to give you both flexibility and real-time support.

Thursday | Online Synchronous

Live Online Discussion

We will discuss AI and machine learning applications in teaching, learning, educational assessment, policy, and academic research.

Tuesday | In Person

Hands-On AI Exploration

Gather in class to actively explore AI tools, collaborate with teammates, and create projects that apply AI to real-world problems.

🚀 Why Take This Course?

  • Play with today’s cutting-edge AI tools
  • Learn through fun, hands-on activities
  • Build future-ready AI skills for your career
  • No coding experience required
  • Low-pressure and highly beginner-friendly
  • Open to students from all majors

✨ Who Should Take It?

Students from education, business, health sciences, arts, social sciences, engineering, and any major curious about how AI is reshaping our world are welcome.

❌ No Traditional Quizzes:
Instead, students work in collaborative teams to develop and propose an AI-based idea to address a real-world problem.
